Top 3 List of Cafe's

Places To Visit After Lockdown

This page includes a list of my top three choices!

1.Sketch Cafe
Sketch Cafe has great interior design to the cafe which is popular among many people to take fancy pictures in the pink cafe room and even the bathrooms.
2.The Lebenese Bakery
The Lebenese bakery has great selection of pastries and pizza's to order and you can choose your own toppings!
3.Elan Cafe London
The flower wall interior is good for pictures, the pretty cakes, pancakes and Lattes are my personal favourite.
*Bonus choice* - Shibuya Cafe London
The cakes, shaved ice desserts and sushi are definitley worth trying!
